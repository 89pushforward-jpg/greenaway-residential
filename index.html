Welcome

Let's get moving!

Pop in to our East Grinstead estate agents and enjoy a warm welcome and a tonne of local knowledge and expertise. Whether you're looking to buy, sell, rent, or let, we have more than twenty years' experience and thousands of property transactions under our belt.

Located close to Ashdown Forest — the legendary home of Winnie the Pooh and the Bluebell Railway — East Grinstead enjoys a reputation for being a great place for families to lay down roots, and a perfect location for commuters within easy reach of London, East Croydon and Gatwick airport.

Get in touch today or pop into our office for a coffee and a friendly chat. We'd love to help you achieve your property ambitions.
