Selling

Having decided to move, selling your current home is probably the most important part of the process — and establishing the right selling price is essential. Too high and buyers stay away; too low and you receive less than you could have achieved.

Marketing

Priced right, marketed wide

When you ask us for advice, we conduct a full market appraisal and talk through what actually matters to you:

  • What is important to you?
  • How quickly do you need to move?
  • What price do you need to achieve to go ahead?
  • What will be included in the sale?

Once we've agreed the price with you, we market your home to the widest audience of genuine buyers — encouraging as many quality viewings as possible, arranged at times convenient for you. Every offer is passed on promptly, fully discussed, and negotiated on your behalf.

Sales progression

The part most agents overlook

Statistically, for every three sales agreed, only two make it to exchange of contracts. Getting you through this stage quickly and efficiently is key to reducing the chances of a sale falling through.

Once you've accepted an offer, we make sure everything proceeds as smoothly as possible — communicating regularly with both solicitors (helping you find one if you wish), the mortgage lenders and every other party in the chain, and most importantly keeping you informed of progress.

It's often where people really see the value of a good estate agent — the work that happens behind the scenes between "offer accepted" and "keys handed over".
