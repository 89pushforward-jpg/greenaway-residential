Buying

Contact us! We want to meet you and find out all about you — the more we understand what you're looking for, the faster we can find it.

Step one

How do I start?

Invest some time and think about the following:

  • Which areas would you consider buying in?
  • Are there other areas you would consider but don't know well?
  • What kind of property are you looking for?
  • What are the "must haves" and "don't wants"?
  • How much are you prepared to spend to get what you want?
  • When do you want to have moved by?
  • Do you need to sell — and if so, is your property on the market yet?
  • Do you know how much you can borrow, and are your finances organised?
Viewing & offering

From first viewing to "I have found it"

I want to see some property

Once we understand your needs, viewing properties helps you get a feel for real market value. Viewing widely builds confidence in pricing — and your honest feedback helps us refine the search until it's right.

I have found it

An offer is about more than price. We'll ask about your buying position, deposit size, mortgage agreement status, timeframes and what's included in the sale — and every offer is confirmed in writing.

What happens now?

Four steps to completion

STEP 1

Instruct a solicitor

We can recommend trusted local firms if you don't have one already.

STEP 2

Mortgage & finances

Contact your IFA or mortgage broker to get your finances moving.

STEP 3

Conveyancing

STEP 4

Exchange & completion

Contracts exchanged, completion date set — and the keys are yours.

This is an exciting yet sometimes stressful time for all involved — our job is to keep you calm and informed through the ups and downs of the transaction.
