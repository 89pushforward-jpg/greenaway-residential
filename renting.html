Renting

If you are looking to rent a property, Greenaway Residential Lettings Agents can help you — across East Grinstead and Crawley, plus Horley, Redhill, Reigate, Horsham, Haywards Heath, Burgess Hill and Crowborough.

How it works

From viewing to moving in

Once you've viewed a property and agreed terms, you'll sign our terms & conditions and pay a holding deposit of one week's rent. All adults over 18 then complete referencing forms — the referencing cost is paid by the landlord, not you.

When referencing is complete, you come back in to sign the Assured Shorthold Tenancy Agreement and pay the remaining monies in cleared funds — then the keys are yours.
